How Did Formula 1 Start

Formula 1 was a concept driven up from the early days of motorsports to create a more hardcore race. However plan to create F1 were stoped to due the start of World War 2. In 1946 the idea of F1 was revitalized and plans to create a more hardcore Drives Championship was start.It took until 1950 for the details to be hammered out.

The First Formula One Event
